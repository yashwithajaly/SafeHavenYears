Basic Elder Exercises

Chair Exercises

Simple exercises to improve strength and flexibility while sitting in a chair.

Walking

A low-impact exercise that helps improve cardiovascular health and leg strength.

Stretching

Gentle stretching exercises to improve flexibility and reduce muscle stiffness.

Breathing Exercises

Deep breathing exercises to promote relaxation and improve lung capacity.

Balance Exercises

Exercises to enhance balance and prevent falls, suitable for seniors.

Seated Leg Lifts

Low-impact leg exercises to strengthen muscles without strain.
